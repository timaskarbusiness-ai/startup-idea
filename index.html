What is Lyrae?

Lyrae is a feedback intelligence platform that brings together reviews, tickets, and community posts in one place, turning them into quantified insights for product growth.

Why do I need this?

Because scattered feedback is hard to trust or act on. Lyrae uncovers the real themes behind user voices, combining context with numbers you can rely on.

Who is this for?

For PMs, growth, CX, and UX teams who need evidence-based decisions.

What can I connect?

App & Play Store reviews, Zendesk, Intercom, Reddit, Twitter, CSV uploads — with insights delivered to Slack, Discord, or email.

Why is it better than interviews or analytics?

Interviews give depth but no scale; analytics give scale but no “why.” Lyrae delivers both.

How is this different from uploading CSVs to an LLM?

LLMs summarize once. Lyrae continuously quantifies signals, tracks trends, and links them to product KPIs — so insights stay reliable over time.
